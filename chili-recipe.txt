Ingredients:
 - 1 Kilo ground beef
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - One 330gm can tomato sauce
 - 2 chillis finely chopped - inc seeds
 - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
 - 1 teaspoon ground oregano
 -1 teaspoon turmeric
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - 80gm corn flour
 - One 330gm can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
 - One 330gm can pinto beans, drained and rinsed

Directions:
 1. Brown the ground beef in a large pot and drain off the excess fat.
 2. Add the tomato sauce, chili, cumin, oregano, salt, and cayenne.
 3. Stir, then cover and simmer over low heat for one hour.
 4. Mix the corn flour and 150ml water in a small bowl.  Add to chili.
 5. Add the beans and simmer for 10 more minutes.
